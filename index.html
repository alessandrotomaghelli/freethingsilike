FREE THINGS

Click the refresh button to discover something you can do for free or almost free.

WHAT IS THIS

This website gives you random suggestions for things you can do that cost nothing or almost nothing.

In a world where everything seems to cost money, there are still plenty of meaningful activities, experiences, and ways to spend your time that are completely free or cost less than the price of a coffee.

WHY DOES THIS EXIST

Because boredom is expensive. Entertainment is expensive. Hobbies are expensive. But they don't have to be.

This is a reminder that fulfillment, learning, creativity, and joy are not locked behind paywalls. They are accessible to everyone, right now, wherever you are.

HOW TO USE IT

Click refresh. Do the thing. Repeat when needed.

Some suggestions require good weather. Some require internet access. Some require other people. Some require nothing but yourself. Take what works for you right now.

CONTRIBUTE

If you know of other great free or almost-free activities, consider contributing to similar projects or sharing your ideas with others who might benefit.
