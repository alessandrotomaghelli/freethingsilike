About

(Free) Things I Like is a very personal collection of everything that, in my opinion, adds great value to life at zero or very low cost (roughly less than the price of a coffee). I have often asked myself which general “things in life,” inter-categorically, could leave a strong impact without requiring significant resources.

Thanks to anyone who has suggested, or will suggest, new ideas at alessandrotomaghelli@gmail.com .

The list will be updated as I go through life and notice new (Free) Things I Like.
